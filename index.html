How the Process Works

Step 1: Consultation - 100% Free

In your initial consultation, we will go over your case, determine what your issues are, and discuss the possible options for resolving your tax problems. The best is it’s absolutely free with no obligation.

Step 2: Investigation

We will establish contact with the IRS and order your master tax file. We review your transcripts and conduct a financial analysis to determine what programs you qualify for. Once this is done we will present you with the results and outline all the resolution options available to you.

Step 3: Resolution

We will work with you to establish IRS compliance and implement the resolution option that works best for you. We will prepare all necessary documents and submit a proposal for IRS approval.

The IRS will accept or reject the proposal. If they reject it, we will work with you to make adjustments and resubmit it. Once the proposal is accepted, your case is closed with your tax troubles behind you.
